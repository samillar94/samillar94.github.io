Welcome to my website!

Working on my degree, I do a lot less stuff than I used to. I used to blog about cool stuff in general and recording and rearing moths in particular, until the blogging bug wore off. I have loads of photos of moths, butterflies and other beasties (and a few videos of their behaviour). I made 83 versions of the Doctor Who theme, and drove my family up the wall with it. Mainly now I curate a customisable Excel periodic table and do the county distributions for the NI micro-moth checklist. And I tweet a bit. That's about it. Happy rummaging! :)
